Why Biscuits Are Better Than Bread

Posted 2 days ago

Biscuits have been around for centuries, offering a delightful crunch and comfort in every bite. Unlike bread, biscuits are quicker to make, often more flavorful, and perfect with tea or coffee.

Whether buttery, sweet, or savory, biscuits adapt to any meal. Their texture, portability, and nostalgic appeal make them a favorite snack across cultures.

So next time you reach for bread, maybe consider a biscuit instead. It might just brighten your day.
